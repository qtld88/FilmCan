# Transfer History

Review past backups and verify data.

---

## Limitations

- History is stored locally on your Mac (not synced).
- Clearing history removes resume and verification context for that backup.

---

## What's Logged

- Time, duration, source, destinations
- Files copied / skipped / failed
- Hash list path (if generated)
- Configuration used

---

## Open History

Click the **clock** icon

---

## Actions

**Check Data**  
Compares destination's files against the saved hashes.

**Use It Again**  
Recreate a backup from a past run.

**Delete history card**  
Remove this history entry.

---

## Filter & Search

- Filter by status (success, failed)
- Search by source, destination, or backup name

---

## Retention

History is kept based on your settings. Delete entries anytime.
